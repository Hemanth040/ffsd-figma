Weed Identification & Control Guide

WHEAT

Phalaris Minor

Little Seed Canary Grass

Wheat

Characteristics:

Grass weed with narrow leaves, purple-tinged stems, and spike-like panicles

Control Methods:

Pre-emergence herbicides, hand weeding, crop rotation

Critical Control Stage:

Early growth stage (2-4 weeks after sowing)

Threat: High Competition: Severe
MULTIPLE CROPS

Cyperus rotundus

Purple Nutsedge

Multiple Crops

Characteristics:

Perennial sedge with triangular stems, dark green leaves, and underground tubers

Control Methods:

Post-emergence herbicides, mulching, soil solarization

Critical Control Stage:

Before tuber formation (4-6 weeks after emergence)

Threat: High Competition: Severe
RICE

Echinochloa crus-galli

Barnyardgrass

Rice

Characteristics:

Tall grass with flat leaves, purple-tinged stems, and spreading panicles

Control Methods:

Pre-emergence herbicides, water management, hand weeding

Critical Control Stage:

Early vegetative stage (2-3 weeks after transplanting)

Threat: High Competition: Severe

Fertilizer Guide & Application Methods

🌱

NITROGEN

Urea

Nitrogen

Formula: CO(NH₂)₂

Nutrients: 46% N
Purpose: Promotes vegetative growth, leaf development, and protein synthesis
Suitable Crops: All crops - Wheat, Rice, Maize, Vegetables
Application Method: Broadcasting, Top dressing, Basal application
Dosage: 50-75 kg/acre (125-187 kg/ha)
Application Timing: Split application - Basal + Top dressing
Suitable Soil: All soil types
🌿

PHOSPHORUS

DAP (Diammonium Phosphate)

Phosphorus

Formula: (NH₄)₂HPO₄

Nutrients: 18% N, 46% P₂O₅
Purpose: Root development, flowering, seed formation, and energy transfer
Suitable Crops: Cereals, Pulses, Oilseeds, Vegetables
Application Method: Basal application, Banding, Drilling
Dosage: 40-50 kg/acre (100-125 kg/ha)
Application Timing: At sowing or transplanting
Suitable Soil: Alkaline to neutral soils
🍂

POTASSIUM

MOP (Muriate of Potash)

Potassium

Formula: KCl

Nutrients: 60% K₂O
Purpose: Water regulation, enzyme activation, disease resistance, and crop quality
Suitable Crops: All crops - Cotton, Sugarcane, Tobacco, Fruits
Application Method: Basal application, Split application
Dosage: 25-40 kg/acre (62-100 kg/ha)
Application Timing: At planting or split during growth
Suitable Soil: All soil types, especially light sandy soils
⚗️

COMPLEX FERTILIZERS

NPK 10-26-26

Complex

N-P₂O₅-K₂O

Nutrients: 10% N, 26% P₂O₅, 26% K₂O
Purpose: Balanced nutrition for vegetative and reproductive growth
Suitable Crops: Pulses, Oilseeds, Fruits, Vegetables
Application Method: Basal application, Drilling
Dosage: 50-100 kg/acre (125-250 kg/ha)
Application Timing: At sowing or transplanting
Suitable Soil: All soil types
🔬

MICRONUTRIENTS

Zinc Sulphate

Micronutrient

Formula: ZnSO₄.7H₂O

Nutrients: 21% Zn, 11% S
Purpose: Prevents zinc deficiency, improves enzyme function and growth
Suitable Crops: Rice, Wheat, Cotton, Citrus, Fruits
Application Method: Soil application, Foliar spray, Seed treatment
Dosage: 10-25 kg/acre (25-62 kg/ha)
Application Timing: At planting or as foliar spray
Suitable Soil: Calcareous and sandy soils
🌾

ORGANIC FERTILIZERS

Vermicompost

Organic

Organic matter

Nutrients: 1-3% N, 0.5-1% P, 0.5-1% K
Purpose: Improves soil structure, water retention, and microbial activity
Suitable Crops: All crops - Vegetables, Fruits, Flowers
Application Method: Soil application, Mixing with potting mix
Dosage: 2-5 tons/acre (5-12.5 tons/ha)
Application Timing: At planting or before sowing
Suitable Soil: All soil types

Disease & Treatment Guide

Leaf Rust

Wheat

Symptoms:

Orange-brown pustules on leaves

Treatment:

Apply fungicide, remove infected plants

Bacterial Blight

Rice

Symptoms:

Water-soaked lesions on leaves

Treatment:

Use resistant varieties, copper-based spray

Corn Smut

Corn

Symptoms:

Gray-black galls on ears and stalks

Treatment:

Remove infected plants, crop rotation
